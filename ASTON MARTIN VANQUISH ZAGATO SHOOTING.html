ASTON MARTIN VANQUISH ZAGATO SHOOTING

Overview

- The long collaboration between Aston Martin and Zagato has led to some of the most exciting cars ever made. However, few if any are as intriguing or appealing as the Vanquish Zagato Shooting Brake. The final instalment of a four-part collection of Vanquish-based Zagato models, the Shooting Brake is a high-performance estate car with an exotic and extraordinary twist. Just 99 cars were produced worldwide. The base came from Aston Martin's flagship Vanquish S, featuring a 5.9L naturally aspirated V12 which produces close to 600BHP, coupled to the 8-speed ZF Touchtronic III gearbox. The bodywork designed by Zagato took over 2000 hours to craft, using lightweight hand-rolled aluminum and carbon fiber panels, leading to some beautiful and functional body accents and a truly iconic design.
