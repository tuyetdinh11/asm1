Aston Martin Vanquish Zagato V12

Overview

- Introducing the Aston Martin Vanquish Zagato Volante, a remarkable masterpiece brought to you by the enduring and legendary partnership between Aston Martin and Zagato. This extraordinary collaboration, dating back to 1960 with the iconic DB4 GT Zagato, has produced a limited production run of highly exclusive vehicles. Amari Supercars proudly presents the latest addition to this prestigious lineage, a breathtaking 2018 Vanquish enhanced with the esteemed Villa D'Este pack.

- Limited to just 99 units worldwide, this Aston Martin Vanquish Zagato Volante is a rare gem. Adorned with brushed metal plaques displaying its exclusive number, '7 of 99', this car exudes exclusivity. The carbon fibre exterior has been meticulously re-sculpted, granting the already stunning Vanquish a more well-rounded and captivating appearance. Flowing lines and distinctive features, including the striking 3D 'Z' pattern front grille and the Vulcan-inspired 'blade' tail lights, elevate the Zagato's road presence to awe-inspiring levels. Underneath the captivating exterior, performance enhancements abound. The magnificent 5.9L V12 engine has been optimized for increased power, complemented by a dynamic suspension setup and lightweight cast magnesium door structures.

- In addition to its remarkable features, it is worth noting that the Villa D'Este pack, available for the Aston Martin Vanquish Zagato Volante, is a highly desirable option. Priced at £20,000, this exclusive package further enhances the car's allure and rarity. Only a select few have been supplied to the United Kingdom, making it an even more sought-after and distinctive offering. By opting for the Villa D'Este pack, fortunate owners of this Vanquish Zagato Volante can elevate their driving experience with an exquisite blend of style, sophistication, and prestige that is truly unparalleled. Amari Supercars invites you to seize this extraordinary opportunity to own a true work of art that embodies the very essence of automotive excellence.
