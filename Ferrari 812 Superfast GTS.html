Ferrari 812 Superfast GTS

Overview

Introducing the awe-inspiring 2022 Ferrari 812 Superfast GTS, now available at Amari Supercars. Dressed in the captivating Blu Tour De France exterior, this iconic beauty effortlessly captures attention wherever it roams. As you enter the cabin, prepare to be enveloped in pure opulence with the meticulously crafted Cuoio Alcantara and Hide interior, a harmonious blend of sophistication and comfort.

Inside the cockpit, an exquisite blend of luxury and sportiness awaits. The Daytona-style seats, adorned with sumptuous leather upholstery and detailed Blu Medio stitching, beckon you to sink in and experience true comfort. Complementing the overall theme are the Blu Sterling leather accents on the centre console and upper dashboard, exuding an air of sophistication. The dash inserts in carbon fibre and the gleaming titanium exhaust pipes subtly hint at the awe-inspiring performance that lies within.
