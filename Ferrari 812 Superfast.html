Ferrari 812 Superfast

Overview

- Introducing the captivating 2020 Ferrari 812 Superfast, available now at Amari Supercars. This sleek beauty commands attention with its stunning Nero Daytona exterior, exuding a sense of power and elegance. Step inside and be greeted by the luxurious Nero Hide interior, enveloping you in comfort and sophistication. The Nero mats perfectly complement the interior, completing the seamless aesthetic.

- This extraordinary machine is not only a sight to behold but also boasts an array of carbon fiber enhancements, showcasing its commitment to lightweight performance. The front part of the front bumper, rear side bumper, diffuser, underdoor cover, engine panels, center tunnel, air intake, inner door handle, driver zone, central bridge, and hub caps are all meticulously crafted from carbon fiber, delivering both exquisite design and enhanced aerodynamics. The carbon fiber inserts on the dash and tricolour dreamline on the F1 paddles add a touch of sporty elegance to the interior, while the Cavallino stitched on the headrest in Rosso Ferrari adds a bold statement.

- Equipped with attention to detail, this Ferrari 812 Superfast features black tailpipe tips, tricolour F1 button housings, and a carbon fibre front spoiler, further accentuating its aggressive stance. The passenger display ensures that both driver and companion are engaged and informed throughout the exhilarating journey. The Rosso Ferrari piping on the seat sides and the yellow rev counter provide a striking contrast against the Nero Hide interior, enhancing the Ferrari's vibrant personality. Completing the ensemble are the 20" forged diamond wheels, adding a touch of sophistication and ensuring an exceptional driving experience.
